Karniyarik

Turkish food called Karniyarik is a delicious aubergine food with meat

    INGREDİDENTS
  • 4 Aubergines
  • 200gr Mince
  • 2 Spoon Tomato Paste
  • 1 Spoon Oil
  • 1 Onion(big)
  • 2 Tomatos
  • 5 Green Peppers
    HOW TO MAKE
  1. Cut the onions into cubes
  2. Add oil,onions and mince to the pan and cook for 30 minutes
  3. Split the middle of the aubergines and stuff the mince in it
  4. Put the aubergines in the oven and turn it on 180 Celcius degrees
  5. THAT'S IT! THE KARNIYARIK IS READY TO EAT. ENJOY YOUR MEAL
