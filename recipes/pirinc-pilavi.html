PİRİNC PİLAVİ

Pirinc Pilavi is the famous and perfect rice food in Turkey


    Ingredidents
  • 500 gr rice
  • 2 spoons of oils
  • Sufficient amount of water


    Recipe
  • Wash the rice with warm water
  • Add oil to the pot, then put the rice
  • Cook them about 10 minutes and add the water until it exceeds the height
  • Cook apprxlmy 20 minutes more
